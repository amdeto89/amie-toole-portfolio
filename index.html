Hi, I'm Amie. I'm a Web Developer.

My Story

I grew up in Phoenix, Arizona and went to college in New York where I studied Politics, Philosophy, and Economics. Determined to have the true New York experience, I interned at W magazine in the Adveritsing and Marketing departments, and then paid my dues to the fashion world by working with a stylist. I shopped, steamed, packed, unpacked, and re-packed clothes for people like Nicole Kidman, Carrie Underwood, and Coco Rocha.


After school, I worked at an ad agency on the Kraft Foods account. Tired of always choosing between paying for laundry or happy hour, I moved back to Arizona. I continued working in publishing and advertising until I decided to jump into Sales for a SaaS tech company based out of Mountain View, California.

I spend my free time coding, cooking Japanese food, and working on my golf swing. My favorite TV shows are Scandal, The Blacklist, Suits, and Nashville. The last good book I read was The Rosie Project. My dream job would be to create real-life spy gadgets while wearing Valentino.
